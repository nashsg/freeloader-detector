The Group Project Freeloader Detective (Theme: Student)

The Real-Life Problem: Every poly and uni student hates group project freeloaders—the phantom teammates who do zero work but take a share of the "A" grade. It is incredibly hard to prove to your lecturer exactly who slacked off without drama.

The Gamified Concept: An automated "Among Us" style detective game for group assignments. It scans your project's digital footprint and exposes the "Impostors" who didn't contribute.

How it works with the AI Stack:

Daytona: Spins up a secure container sandbox to safely download and read your group project's folder, code repositories, or shared file histories.

Kimi AI: Acts as the Lead Detective. It runs a script inside Daytona to check the metadata logs (who edited which file, who wrote how many lines, and when they did it). If a teammate only typed two sentences at 3:00 AM the night before the deadline, Kimi flags them.

SenseNova U1: Natively packages the audit logs into a clean, visual "Contribution Graph" or an downloadable PDF "Evidence Dossier" that you can anonymously drop into your professor's grading portal.
